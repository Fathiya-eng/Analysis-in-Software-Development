Hospital Management System – Analysis Phase Example
1. System Overview

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is designed to manage hospital operations efficiently, including patient management, appointments, doctor schedules, billing, pharmacy, lab tests, and reporting. The goal is to improve patient care, streamline workflows, and maintain accurate records.

Key Modules:

Patient Management

Doctor/Staff Management

Appointment Scheduling

Billing & Payments

Pharmacy Management

Laboratory Management

Reporting & Analytics

Notifications & Alerts

4. Acceptance Criteria Example

Here’s a detailed format we can use for acceptance criteria (already included in the table above):

Given [precondition]

When [action is performed]

Then [expected outcome]

Example for US02 – Book Appointment:

Given a patient is logged in

When they select a doctor and time slot

Then the appointment is confirmed, stored, and the patient receives a notification
